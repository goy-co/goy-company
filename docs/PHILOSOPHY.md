# Philosophy // Brutalist Luxury & Sovereignty

The Goy Company operates under a strict set of design and ethical principles. We do not build for the masses; we build for those who demand efficiency, power, and absolute control over their digital existence.

## 1. Brutalist Luxury
Our aesthetic is **Brutalist**. It is raw, functional, and unapologetic. We use hard edges, monospaced typography, and high-contrast color palettes (Strictly Green for Uplink, Red for Termination).

Luxury in our context is defined by **efficiency and exclusivity**. A product is luxurious if it respects the user's time and intelligence. No marketing fluff, no rounded corners, no unnecessary animations. Only the signal matters.

## 2. Zero Data Capture
The Goy Company behaves like a hardware terminal. 
- **No Tracking**: We do not use analytics or third-party trackers.
- **No Cookies**: We only use `sessionStorage` and local session cookies for authentication.
- **Ephemeral State**: Whenever possible, the system clears state on termination. 

## 3. Radical Sovereignty
We believe that identity should not be granted by a central authority.
- **Nostr as the Root**: Our primary identity layer is Nostr. You own your keys; you own your identity.
- **Self-Custody**: Financial systems are built on Liquid/Bitcoin protocols to ensure no one can freeze your assets.

## 4. The Hybrid Bridge
While we champion sovereignty, we recognize the need for accessibility. We provide a "Portal Access" path for traditional users, allowing them to participate in the ecosystem while constantly offering a path to "Export to Nostr"—the ritual of becoming truly sovereign.
